Would You Survive the Hunger Games?

May the odds be ever in your favor. Create a Canva presentation that explains your tribute profile, survival strategy, and whether you would actually survive in the arena.
Your Canva must have at least 6 slides. Use complete sentences, clear explanations, and visuals.

Slide 1: Tribute Profile

  • Your tribute name
  • Your age
  • Your district
  • A picture, avatar, or symbol for your tribute
  • Your tribute score from 1–12

Tribute Score

Before the Games begin, the Gamemakers watch each tribute show off their skills. They give each tribute a score from 1 to 12. This score tells the audience, sponsors, and other tributes how dangerous you might be.

  • 1–3: Weak tribute. Others may think you are easy to defeat.
  • 4–6: Average tribute. You have some useful skills.
  • 7–9: Strong tribute. You are considered dangerous.
  • 10–12: Elite tribute. You are one of the favourites to win.

Explain why you earned your score. What skill impressed the Gamemakers?

Slide 2: Strengths and Weaknesses

  • Choose 3 strengths that would help you survive.
  • Choose 2 weaknesses that could hurt your chances.
  • Explain each choice.
Examples of strengths: running, climbing, hunting, hiding, leadership, intelligence, first aid, swimming, throwing, staying calm, making friends, finding food, building shelters.
Examples of weaknesses: fear, poor teamwork, bad aim, no survival skills, too trusting, not athletic, panic under pressure, afraid of heights, loud, impatient.

Slide 4: Arena Strategy

  • What would you do when the Games begin?
  • Would you run from the Cornucopia or grab supplies?
  • Where would you hide or camp?
  • How would you find food and water?
  • Would you fight early or avoid people?
Your strategy should match your strengths, weapon, and supplies.

Slide 5: Alliances and Sponsors

  • Would you join an alliance?
  • What kind of tribute would you trust?
  • Would you betray your alliance, leave peacefully, or stay loyal?
  • What sponsor gift would you hope to receive?
  • What would you do to earn sponsor support?
Sponsor gift examples: medicine, food, water, blanket, rope, extra arrows, fire starter, bandages.

Slide 6: Final Reflection

  • Would you actually survive? Why or why not?
  • What would be your biggest advantage?
  • What would be your biggest challenge?
  • Give yourself a survival rating out of 10.
Use complete sentences. Be honest. A strong answer does not have to say you would win.
